Postdoctoral Scholars

January 16, 2020 Jingjie Yeo

We are committed in working with prospective scholars to apply for postdoctoral fellowships in the following research areas:

• Synthetic biology pertaining to the human microbiome
 • Metallic alloys and ceramics for 3D printing/additive manufacturing
 • Dynamically-responsive and/or functional polymeric or biopolymeric materials
Comprehensive lists of funding sources are available HERE and HERE. Some possible funding sources include:
• Helen Hay Whitney Foundation
 • NIH Individual Postdoctoral Fellowships (F32)
 • NSF Postdoctoral Research Fellowships in Biology
 • Cornell Presidential Postdoctoral Fellowship
For Singaporeans in particular, potential postdoctoral fellowships include:
• Nanyang Technological University CoE MOE Talent Development Scheme
 • A*STAR International Fellowship
 • A*STAR Graduate Scholarship (Post-Doc)

Postgraduate Students

January 16, 2020 Jingjie Yeo

For potential postgraduate students, please consider applying to Cornell University's graduate school program. The deadline for the following year's Fall admission is on Dec 15th annually. We are also committed in working with potential students in applying for graduate fellowships, including:
• NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)
 • National Defense Science and Engineering (NDSEG)
Cornell University and our research group are dedicated to enhancing diversity and inclusion in STEM education. Various diversity fellowships are available to graduate students under the Diversity Programs in Engineering (DPE).

For Singaporeans, potential graduate fellowships include:
• National Science Scholarship
 • A*STAR Graduate Scholarship

Undergraduate Researchers

January 16, 2020 Jingjie Yeo

We strongly encourage undergraduate researchers to apply for research opportunities within our research group. Further information can be found HERE. In particular, Cornell University's Engineering Learning Initiatives administers the Student Grant Program for semester or summer funding awards to support undergraduate research engagement.

Cornell University and our research group are dedicated to enhancing diversity and inclusion in STEM education. Cornell Engineering’s Office of Diversity Programs in Engineering (DPE) administers the Louis Stokes Alliance for Minority Participation (LSAMP) Summer Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) and we welcome any potential applicants to reach out to us.
